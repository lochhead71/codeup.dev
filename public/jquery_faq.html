Jellystone

  • Friendly bears
  • Uptight rangers
  • Scenic picnic spots
  • Pick-a-nick baskets often disappear

Yosemite

  • Short men of Scotch-Irish descent are abundant
  • They may have short tempers (pun intended)
  • They often are well-armed
  • Negotiations are expedited by offering surplus ammunition

Big Bend

  • Located in far southwest Texas
  • Named after a feature in the Rio Grande river
  • Has multpile enviornments including alpine and desert
  • Wildlife include brown bears, coyote and mountain lions
